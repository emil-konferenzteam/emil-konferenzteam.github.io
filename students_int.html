Tutorial for Eventmobi

All sessions of the Conference Week 2021 are bundled on Eventmobi. During the four days you can pick and choose your own schedule. Here's all the information you need for Eventmobi:

  5. How do sessions with attendee limits work?

1. How do you get to Eventmobi?

The link to our event on Eventmobi is eventmobi.com/vcw21. If you want to use the Eventmobi iOS or Android App you have to enter the code vcw21.

2. How do you login?

Enter the email address you've got this email sent to. It should be your @stud.leuphana.de address. Click "Continue".

Set yourself a password and click "Continue".

If you are asked to enter a password, but don't remeber it (or don't remember having one in the first place), click on "Forgot password". You will receive a email to the address you've just entered with a link to set a new password.

A new tab will open. Set yourself a password and click on "Reset Password". Go back to the first tab.

Enter your new password and click on "Continue".

3. How do you sign up for sessions?

You are already signed up for your seminar plenary, your project group session and the opening event. You have to attend at least 9 sessions. So, counting the aforementioned you have to pick at least 6 additional sessions.

All sessions are listed on the home page, sorted by session type. To take a look of all sessions of a type, click on it's widget. At the top you can change to individual days or view all. For more information on a session, click on it. To sign up for a session, you have to add it to your schedule. To do that, click on "+Add to Schedule".

Only sign up for one session per time slot. If you are signed up for multiple sessions in the same time slot, we will remove you from those sessions.

4. How does the schedule work?

You can find your schedule either as a widget on the bottom of the home page or in the drop down menu under "My Schedule" in the top right corner.

The schedule has two view modes you can change between in the top right corner. In the list view, you can change the days in the drop down menu to the left. Again, you can also view all days at once.

In the table view, you can only view one day at a time. The sessions are sorted by type, not by day.

You are only signed up for a session, if it is added to your schedule. Do not remove sessions from your schedule you want to attend (especially your project group session).

5. How do sessions with attendee limits work?

Some sessions only have a limited capacity for attendees. It's first come, first serve. If a session has a limited capacity, it says so in it's title in brackets and description.

The title updates with a time delay, so it takes a little to show the current capacity, should you add it to your schedule. You may have to reload the page. Because of this time delay, it is possible that more students sign up to a session than there is room in it's capacity. Should this happen to you, please understand that we will remove you from the session and email you about it.

If a session is full, it will no longer show up in the regular listings but can only be found via the schedule by it's attendees. The same goes for closed sessions like your project group session or in some cases your seminar plenary.

6. How do you attend a session?

Every session either takes place in a Zoom session or a YouTube livestream. You can find both links on the session page. To join the Zoom Session, click on the button that says "Join Zoom Session". This will open your Zoom client or the web app.

YouTube livestreams are embedded on the session page.

7. The most important rules for Eventmobi

  • You have to sign up for at least 9 sessions.

    • three of which are your seminar plenary, your project group session and the opening event.

  • Do not remove sessions from your schedule you want to attend.

    • Closed sessions like your project group session and sessions with full capacities cannot be added to your schedule once you remove them. Should you accidently do so, you can contact us at EMAIL or NUMBER.

  • Only sign up for one session per time slot.

    • If you sign up for multiple session in the same time slot, we will remove you from those sessions.

  • You are only signed up for a session, if you have added it to your schedule.
